The Problem

Processors struggle to get consistent, high-quality crops.

Many processors want to produce more, but the raw materials they receive are often wet, damaged, poorly sorted, or inconsistent. This makes production harder, lowers output quality, and reduces how much value can stay in local markets.

Our Solution

We improve the supply before it reaches the processor.

Avrauca works upstream with farmers and suppliers to prepare crops better before they move. This includes better drying, sorting, cleaning, storage, grading, and coordination with processors that are ready to buy higher-quality inputs.

How It Works

Today we solve sourcing. Tomorrow we power procurement.

1. Start with one crop

We focus on crops where quality can drop quickly before processing, such as cocoa, cassava, cashews, shea, sesame, or coffee.

2. Build supplier networks

We work with farmers and suppliers who can provide the raw materials processors need.

3. Improve quality before transport

We help organize drying, sorting, cleaning, storage, and basic quality checks before crops move.

4. Connect supply to demand

We connect better-prepared crops to processors, buyers, and eventually financing partners.

Farmers win. Processors win. Buyers win.

Farmers can earn more from better-prepared crops. Processors receive cleaner and more reliable inputs. Buyers get better products. Avrauca helps make the chain work better from the farm gate to the final sale.
